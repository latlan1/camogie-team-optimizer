Camogie Team Optimization

Split your players into two balanced teams using constraint programming. Upload a CSV with player ratings and let the solver find the fairest division.

How It Works

This tool uses MiniZinc constraint programming to solve a team balancing problem. Given a list of players with skill ratings, it assigns each player to Team A or Team B while:

Objective: minimize |total_rating_A - total_rating_B|

Constraints: Equal team sizes (or differ by at most 1 player)

Scenario: Ratings Only - Teams balanced by total skill rating only.

Available Solvers

  • CBC - Fastest (~150ms)
  • COIN-BC - MIP solver (~330ms)
  • Chuffed - Lazy clause gen (~370ms)
  • CP-SAT - OR-Tools (~440ms)

Gecode is disabled on macOS ARM64 due to a threading bug in the native binary.
